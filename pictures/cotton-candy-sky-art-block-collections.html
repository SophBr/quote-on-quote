Cotton Candy Sky

Artist

Art Block Collections

Medium

Description

I walked out into my backyard and saw these puffy pink clouds in the sky. I know clouds are just condensed water vapor, but in my fanciful mind I saw cotton candy! These beatiful clouds are called cirrocumulus clouds. When the sun is low in the sky like this, they can be dramatic.
